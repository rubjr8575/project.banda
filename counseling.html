COUNSELING

We provide One-On-One Counseling for multiple areas of Life Applications

Image Enhancement

To give clients, who have been living with addiction, a new sense of value and to help them clarify and reawaken the values they already have inside of them

Life Choices

To help clients learn that they are able of make the right choices in life for themselves and their family.

YOUR CHILD AND CHOICES

To assist clients in making better choices for their lives and to learn how to build a better relationship with their children.

HOME MANAGEMENT

To assist clients who, due to addiction, never learned how manage a household by keeping it clean and orderly, or to teach their children how to do the same.

BUDGETING

To teach clients how to manage money. To show them how to manage household expenses such as buying food, clothing, and paying bills while managing to keep a little spending money, too.

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

To teach clients especially women that violence is not love and to show them how to recognize a violent person before they get into a relationship. To help clients understand or identify the root cause of why they are drawn into that kind of relationship.

MARRIAGE COUNSELING

To help resolve marital conflicts that arise due to the addiction of one or both partners, and other problems.
